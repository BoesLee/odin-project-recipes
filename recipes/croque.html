Some Regarded Belgian's
"Winter" Croque

Because Winter means Raclette.

Ingredients

  • Two Slices of Bread
  • Moderate Amount of Raclette Cheese
  • Herbes de Provence, Pepper and Dried Chili Flakes
  • Some Dijon Mustard
  • A Form of Meat
    Note: Preferably some Dry-Cured Meat, but Whatever Will Do
  • Rarely: Butter
    Note: Depends on "Croque-ing" Method

Instructions

  1. Grab One Slice
  2. Add Raclette Cheese
  3. Add Meat
  4. Sprinkle Herbes de Provence, Pepper, Dried Chili Flakes to Taste
  5. Moderately Spread Dijon Mustard over Other Slice
  6. Put "Dijon'd" Side on Toppings
  7. Croque-ing
    • If ( One has Panini Press or Equivalent )
      1. Put Croque in Press
    • If ( One has Pan ... or Pot )
      1. Spread Butter on Outside of Both Slices
      2. Cook Croque
    • If ( One Only has Microwave and Toaster Available )
      1. Toast the Bread
      2. Put Croque in Microwave
      3. Turn Croque Upside Down
  8. Cut Diagonally for No Reason
    Note: Following the Square Bread Cutting Rules
